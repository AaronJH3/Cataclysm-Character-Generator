Cataclysm-Character-Generator

Wanted to make it easier for users to create their own character templates for the game Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead - This mod allows more functionality and customization within the game by using a GUI to make it easier for players to customer their characters within the game.

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Purpose

"Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead (DDA)" is a game that has developed a cult following - I found that the community didn't have an easy way of creating their characters and implementing them easily in the game. My application parses all of this data into a list that is easily legible to the human eye rather than having to deal with the issues of looking back and forth between raw files. Instead GUI brings it all together to the front of the user to make it easy for them to pick and choose the traits and items the player wants their character to have upon creation.
